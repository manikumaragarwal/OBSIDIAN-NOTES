# UNIT 2 : ENTREPRENEURIAL MINDSET

## What is Entrepreneurial Mindset ?

a way of thinking and acting, which help in :

- finding opportunity
- manage uncertainty
- build resilience
- promote innovation
- long term success

### Key Dimension of Entrepreneurial Mindset ?

1. Opportunity orientation
2. Innovativeness
3. Resilience and Perseverance
4. Proactiveness
5. Risk-taking ability
6. Learning objective
7. Ethical Judgement

### Growth Mindset v/s Fixed Mindset (Dweck)

| Properties | Fixed        | Growth            |
| ---------- | ------------ | ----------------- |
| Abilties   | can't change | can change        |
| Challenges | avoids       | Embrace           |
| Failure    | fear them    | Learn from them   |
| Actions    | gives up     | Persistent effort |

## Developing an Entrepreneurial Mindset

1. Learn from Failures

- Analysis mistakes
- adapting strategies

2. Networking

- Learn from peers and seniours

3. Continuous learning

- learn new skills
- market awareness

4. Self Reflection

- Understanding our own weaknesses and strengths

5. Experimental Learning

- Projects
- Internships
- Case studies

#### Ethics Include :

# UNIT 4 : MARKET ANALYSIS & CUSTOMER RESEARCH

## Knowing your CUSTOMER

## Market Segmentation

dividing market into small homogenous groups having similiar types of customers.

### Types of Segmentation

1. Geographical

- Region
- Climate
- Urban/Rural

2. Demographical

- Age
- Gender
- Income
- Education
- Occupation

3. Psychographical

- Lifestyle
- Values
- Personality

4. Behavioural

- Usage rate
- brand loyalty
- buying behaviour

## Market Research

- collecting
- analyzing
- interpreting data

### Primary Research

- data collect form customers directly

### Secondary Research

- data collect from :
  - gov. reports
  - journals
  - industry reports
  - websites

## Customer Value Proposition (CVP)

- why should a customer choose one product over the competitor's product.

### Components:

1. Customer problem
2. product/service solution
3. Unique benefits

## Customer Targeting
